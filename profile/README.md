### What We Do

Somniatore is an ecosystem of applications and microservices designed to help modern businesses scale, automate, and communicate. We specialize in three core pillars:

* 🌐 **Modern Websites**: Premium, high-performance, and beautifully designed user experiences.
* 💬 **Dedicated Business Messaging**: Outbound and inbound SMS routing broker systems.
* 🤖 **24/7 AI-Powered Assistants**: Intelligent, context-aware service assistants designed to automate client inquiries and bookings.
